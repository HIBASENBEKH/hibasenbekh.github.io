ALAPPUZHA

Alappuzha, also known as Alleppey, is a beautiful city located in the southern Indian state of Kerala. It is often referred to as the "Venice of the East" due to its extensive network of picturesque backwaters, canals, and lagoons. Alappuzha is famous for its natural beauty, serene waterways, and unique culture. Alappuzha is renowned for its backwater cruises, which offer a peaceful and scenic way to explore the region. Visitors can take houseboat rides that traverse the interconnected waterways, passing through lush green paddy fields, coconut groves, and quaint villages. The backwaters of Alappuzha are a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Houseboats in Alappuzha are a quintessential Kerala experience. These traditional boats, often beautifully crafted, provide a comfortable and unique accommodation option for tourists. You can enjoy the serene backwaters while relishing delicious Kerala cuisine served on board. It is a charming destination that offers a unique blend of natural beauty, cultural richness, and a tranquil atmosphere. It is a must-visit for travelers seeking a peaceful and picturesque getaway in the backwaters of Kerala.
