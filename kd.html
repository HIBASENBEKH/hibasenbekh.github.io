KOZHIKODE

Kozhikode, also known as Calicut, is a coastal city located in the Indian state of Kerala. It is historically significant and known for its vibrant culture, beautiful beaches, and rich culinary heritage Indian history as it was the first place where Vasco da Gama, the Portuguese explorer, landed in India in 1498, marking the beginning of European maritime trade with India.Kozhikode has a thriving cultural scene with art galleries, music festivals, and traditional dance performances. The city celebrates various festivals with great enthusiasm, including Onam, Eid, and Christmas. Kozhikode is known for its educational institutions, including the Indian Institute of Management Kozhikode (IIMK) and the National Institute of Technology Calicut (NITC). Kozhikode is a city that beautifully blends history, culture, natural beauty, and modern development. Whether you're interested in exploring historical landmarks, indulging in delicious cuisine, or simply relaxing on its scenic beaches, Kozhikode offers a diverse range of experiences for travelers.
