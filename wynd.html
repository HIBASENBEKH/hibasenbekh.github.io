WAYANAD

Wayanad is a picturesque district located in the Indian state of Kerala. Nestled in the Western Ghats, it is known for its lush green landscapes, misty hills, and rich biodiversity. Wayanad is a popular destination for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers. The district is home to dense forests, wildlife sanctuaries, and pristine rivers, making it an ideal place for trekking, wildlife safaris, and outdoor exploration. Visitors can experience the serene beauty of places like Chembra Peak, Pookode Lake, Banasura Sagar Dam, and the Edakkal Caves. Wayanad also has a rich cultural heritage, with indigenous tribal communities, traditional festivals, and unique cuisine that reflects the region's diverse influences. Whether you're seeking a peaceful retreat in nature or a taste of authentic Kerala culture, Wayanad offers a delightful escape in the lap of nature.
